Tendon-driven Robotics

Pulley-guided tendon-driven continuum arm

A continuum robotic arm architecture focused on reducing tendon actuation effort while preserving stiffness.

Overview

This project investigates how tendon routing and mechanical architecture affect the performance of compliant continuum robots.

Design approach

The design uses a two-stage soft/continuum structure with pulley-guided routing so tendon forces reorient more efficiently through the arm.
