Shape-morphing tensegrity structures

Soft robotics and programmable compliance

A shape-morphing tensegrity project exploring programmable compliance through tensegrity, jamming, and pneumatic actuation. The work uses mechanism design to connect deployable structures with soft robotic behavior.

Project framing

  • Problem-driven mechanical design with rapid build-test iteration.
  • Emphasis on geometry, material behavior, and manufacturable implementation.
